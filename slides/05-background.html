Summary

  • We extended the original static SimIIR framework by developing SimIIR 2.0 to support dynamic, history-aware simulations.
  • This work prompted renewed community interest and several independent extensions, motivating the development of the unified SimIIR 3.0.
  • SimIIR 3 modernizes the framework for the era of LLMs, adding support for:
    • LLM-Powered simulated users.
    • Conversational Search simulations.
    • Integration with modern backends.
